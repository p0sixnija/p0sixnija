It's time to protect your ideas and investments

Blockchain, like all emerging technology, is currently unregulated in many nations, particularly in its early phases. However, this does not imply that it works in a legal vacuum: because our legal system is comprehensive, the rights and obligations that it entails also apply to the chain of blocks.

BlockFIRM recognizes this context and provides specialized advise based on the existing regulatory framework, adapted to each instance and in accordance with not just the development of this technology, but also the rules that apply to it.
